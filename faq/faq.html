Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rock Guitar Techniques?

RGT is a resource to help you build a roadmap of what rock guitar techniques to learn. Each technique page provides you with tips, trivia and 12 tabs of songs where the technique is used (or songs that are good to practice the technique).

Are the techniques ordered by difficulty?

Not exactly, well.. Kind of.. I would say I ordered them by their usefulness and how common they are considering the effort to learn them.

What do I need to be able to use the tabs?

I have downloaded the tabs for each technique and converted them to .gp5 (guitar pro 5) for your convenience since it remains the most common version out there. So, you need guitar pro 5+ or TuxGuitar which is free, here's a link: Click here.

Can I learn all this stuff on my own?

Absolutely, it's how I + many many rock fans like you did it. I personally learned faster by going straight into songs and by watching how others played them. Exercises are great too, if they don't put you to sleep, for me they mostly do. That is why I set this site up as a tool for people more geared towards practical learning.

The 'songs' from the main page are so cool! Where are they from?
