WTH is RockGuitarTechniques.com?

Hey there, fellow guitar and rock enthusiasts! Welcome to RockGuitarTechniques.com, a passion project and your roadmap for guitar techniques to learn on your journey to rock.

Of course, exercises are great to learn techniques. However, who hasn't started learning a technique and thought "Man, I wish I knew more songs that use this!". On every technique you click, RockGuitarTechniques.com displays 12 songs from varied styles with downloadable guitar pro tabs (all rights and credits reserved to original owners in tab info).

On each technique, you will see theoretical info, learning tips and trivia about the technique. I have collected for you hundreds of songs on the website along with good covers on YouTube so that you can also see the technique as it is performed by a cool guitarist (all credits to the YouTube rockers involved and tagged).

Whether you're a beginner, or prime Van Halen, hopefully this website will be a good learning resource and a collection of songs helping you put your knowledge into practice. Rock on,
